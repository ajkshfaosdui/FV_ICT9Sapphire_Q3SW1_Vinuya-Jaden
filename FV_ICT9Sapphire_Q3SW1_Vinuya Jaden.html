YOUR LIE IN APRIL

OVERVIEW/INTRODUCTION

A piano prodigy who lost his ability to play after suffering from a traumatic childhood and was forced back into playing with the help of a girl he met in a park to fight back the fear.

CHARACTERS

Kousei Arima

Kousei Arima is the main protagonist. He used to play the piano until he had lost his mother, leaving him with a traumatic childhood that prevents him hearing his own playing.

Kaori Miyazono

Kaori Miyazono was the main female protagonist who eventually met Arima Kousei in a park. She is a violinist who helped Arima Kousei play the piano again.

Tsubaki Sawabe

Tsubaki Sawabe is Kousei's childhood friend. She was a baseball player who had been supporting her from the start they have met. Often would go to his performances, cheered him on and would invite Kousei to play outside. She acted as an older sister to Kousei knowing how his mother was abusive.

Ryota Watari

Watari was a close friend of Tsubaki and Kousei. A captain of a football team. He regularly spent time together with them and cared for them. He was very approachable and kind to everyone.
